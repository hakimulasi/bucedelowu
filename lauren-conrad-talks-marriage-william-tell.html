Lauren Conrad talks marriage with husband-of-one-month William Tell

July 2024 Â· 2 minute read

    Lauren Conrad has opened up about married life with William Tell. The fashion designer and her lawyer love tied the knot exactly a month ago, but it seems the couple didn't feel much change after their nuptials. "It doesn't feel too different," Lauren told . "We felt married before we were actually married."

    William Tell and Lauren Conrad first met on Valentine's Day

    Pointing to his new wedding band, Will, 34, added: "I could get used to this." The newlyweds, who first met on Valentine's Day in 2012 and started dating soon after, kicked off their honeymoon with a romantic getaway in Sicily. The pair travelled around the countryside, taking in the breathtaking landscape, with Lauren revealing that her favourite part of the island was the food. "We just had amazing meals everywhere," she said. "We rented a car and drove around, it was a beautiful place." Lauren shared one of her most memorable dinners with her 3.6million fans on Instagram, posting a photo of herself holding up a gargantuan pizza and sipping a glass of red wine. "Post wedding diet," she captioned it.

    Lauren Conrad shared a photo of her post-wedding diet

    The former The Hills star, 28, looked to be having the time of her life as she wrote alongside another snap of an Italian phrasebook, "How do you say 'I may never come home?'".Lauren and Will then travelled to Santorini, Greece for the second part of their European honeymoon. The couple made their first high-profile appearance as Mr and Mrs Tell over the weekend, when they attended the 5th Annual Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic party in Lauren's home state of California.

    Lauren Conrad and William Tell married in California last month

    The blonde beauty and her new husband were pictured cosying up together as they showed off their wedding bands. Soaking up the sunshine, Lauren channelled girly chic in a floral blouse and high-waisted navy skirt. The dress designer, who is known for her impeccable taste, seems to be working on her new marital home with Will as she recently shared a photo of a hand painted tile that read "Villa Tell". The couple got engaged in October of last year and tied the knot on a Santa Ynez, California wine estate in mid-September.
