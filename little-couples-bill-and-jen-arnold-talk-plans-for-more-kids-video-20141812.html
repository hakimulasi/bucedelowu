Little Couple's Bill and Jen Arnold Talk Plans for More Kids: Video

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Two is plenty of company for The Little Couple‘s Bill and Jen Arnold. The TLC reality stars already have their hands full with a 4-year-old son, Will, and a 3-year-old daughter, Zoey, and they’re not looking to add any more to the brood anytime soon.

“We’re pretty good with two,” Bill revealed when he and his wife stopped by Us Weekly‘s New York City offices recently. “Two kids is, I think, our max for the moment.”

Then, joking, he added: “Once they’re like 10 and they hate both of us and want to be on their own, we’ll go adopt another child or we’ll go get a baby mama or something and try it again at 50.”
