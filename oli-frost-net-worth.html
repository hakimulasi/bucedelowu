The Satirical Symphony of Oli Frost

Born in 1991, Oli Frost, widely known by his stage name Recorda Boi, has carved a niche in the British satire and music scene. His journey is not just a tale of tunes but a saga of striking societal chords with humor and innovation.

Early Musical Notes and Britain’s Got Talent

Frost’s career began with a bang on the national stage of Britain’s Got Talent, where he introduced audiences to his alter ego, Recorda Boi.

This persona, armed with a recorder and a knack for hip-hop beats, wasn’t just about reviving a childhood instrument—it was about challenging cultural norms. This appearance laid the foundation for his popularity and was a significant first step in building his brand.
