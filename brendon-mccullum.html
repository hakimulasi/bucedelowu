Brendon McCullum is a former New Zealand cricket player who is widely considered to be one of the finest batsmen and captains of the New Zealand cricket team. He represented his national side from 2002 until 2016 and set many records like scoring the the fastest ever Test century (in 54 balls), among several others. Under his captaincy, the New Zealand team reached the finals of the ICC World Cup for the first time in 2015.
