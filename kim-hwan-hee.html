Kim Hwan-hee is a South Korean actor who started her career as a child artist and has been cast in several shows like Oh My Ghost, Here’s My Plan, Reply 1997, When the Weather Is Fine, On the Way to the Airport, Adolescence Medley, Beautiful World, Ohlala Couple, The Miracle We Met, Sweet Revenge, Angel Eyes, You Are the Best!, My One and Only, Invincible Lee Pyung Kang, Believe in Love, Robber, Don’t Cry My Love, and You’re Here, You’re Here, You’re Really Here. Kim Hwan-hee Facts

  • For her performances in both Believe in Love and My One and Only, she won the 2011 KBS Drama Awards for “Best Young Actress”.
  • Kim Hwan-hee was seen as Moon Bo-ri in the 2013 comedy-drama film Born to Sing. It is based on National Singing Contest, an actual TV show that became the longest-running Sunday TV program in Korea since its premiere in 1980.
  • In 2013, she was cast as Han Woo-joo, Hye-shin’s daughter, in the family comedy romantic drama series You Are the Best!. She received a nomination at the KBS Drama Awards in the “Best Young Actress” category.
  • Kim Hwan-hee made a cameo appearance as Yoon Chae-hee in an episode of the romantic thriller comedy-drama series Oh My Ghost in 2015. The show stars Park Bo-young, Jo Jung-suk, Lim Ju-hwan, and Kim Seul-gi.
  • She appeared as Hyo-jin, Jong-goo’s daughter, in the 2016 horror film The Wailing, starring Kwak Do-won, Hwang Jung-min, and Chun Woo-hee. This role won her the 53rd Grand Bell Award as well as the Korea Film Actors Association Award in the “Best New Actress” category while she was also nominated at the 37th Blue Dragon Film Awards and the 53rd Baeksang Arts Awards.
  • She brought to life the character of Park Hyo-eun, Soo-ah and Jin-seok’s daughter, in the romantic melodrama television series On the Way to the Airport in 2016 and this work got her nominated for a KBS Drama Award in the “Best Young Actress” category.
  • In 2018, Kim Hwan-hee played the role of Song Ji-soo in the fantasy romantic drama television series The Miracle We Met and this performance won her the KBS Drama Award for “Best Young Actress”.
  • Kim Hwan-hee co-starred as Lee So-hyun, a young woman who drops out of high school and lives by pick-pocketing to support herself, in the 2021 romantic drama television series Here’s My Plan. This won her the MBC Drama Award in the “Excellence Award, Actress in a Short Drama” category.
