The Genesis of a Music Maestro

Mr Jukes, known in his earlier life as Jack Steadman, embarked on a musical odyssey that began with the indie rock sensation Bombay Bicycle Club. His role as the vocalist and primary songwriter for the band since its formation in 2005 marked the inception of his illustrious career.

The Leap into Solo Waters

The year 2016 witnessed a pivotal turn when Bombay Bicycle Club announced a hiatus. This moment was not just a pause but a launchpad for Steadman, who embraced his solo persona, Mr Jukes. The transformation was not just nominal; it was a deep dive into a world of jazz, soul, and funk, distinct from his indie rock roots.

Crafting Music on the High Seas

Jukes’ debut album God First is not just an album; it’s a testament to creativity. Conceived in the unlikely confines of a cabin aboard a cargo ship, this album weaves the influences of his global journey and his reading of Joseph Conrad’s Typhoon into its very fabric.

Collaborations and Musical Journeys

Who is Mr Jukes?

He is the stage name of Jack Steadman, primarily known as the vocalist and primary songwriter of the indie rock band Bombay Bicycle Club. He began his solo career under this name.

Why did he choose the name Mr Jukes?

The name is inspired by a character named Jukes from Joseph Conrad’s novella Typhoon. Steadman chose this name while he was travelling on a cargo ship, reminiscent of the character in the novella.

His music is known for its blend of jazz, soul, and funk. His works often include collaborations with other artists and are known for their sample-heavy, genre-blurring style.
