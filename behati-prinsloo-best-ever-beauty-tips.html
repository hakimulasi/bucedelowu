GALLERY: Behati Prinsloo's best ever beauty tips

July 2024 · 2 minute read

    Whether she's turning heads on the Oscars red carpet or flaunting her incredible figure at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show, Behati Prinsloo never fails to wow her fans with her beauty looks and fitness routine. 

    Luckily for us, the star often reveals her secrets – so we've rounded up the top 10 health and beauty tips she's shared…

    On her daily beauty routine:

    "It's very simple; I wash my face every morning and night, I wear sunblock every day, and I use a good cream and eye cream at night," Behati told .

    On staying in shape before the Victoria's Secret show:

    "I have been eating a lot of protein and vegetables instead, and drinking lots of water," the Namibia-born model told . "I still eat chocolate sometimes, and I did have a few French fries before the show! My favourite treat, though, is definitely Haribos, I love them."

    On make-up:

    "I don't really wear much makeup on a daily basis—I keep it minimal," she told . "I wish I could give myself a cat eye, but I fail every time! Instead, I keep it simple and natural."

    On travel beauty:

    "The hardest part is flying. The air is drying, so during the flight, I love rosewater sprays, Elizabeth Arden's Eight Hour Cream, and Weleda Skin Food, which is so thick and hydrating," Behati revealed to . "And then on location, I'm always in the sun, so sunblock is the most important thing."

    On good skincare:

    "I drink a lot of water and always wear sunblock. I also make sure to get my beauty sleep. On top of that I treat myself by going to the spa and having masks and oxygen facials," the supermodel explained to .

    On working out:

    "My workout routines are constant as I always have to keep fit and stay toned," Behati told . "I do a mixture of Pilates, yoga and working with my personal trainer who does more cross fit for me before I hit the runway."

    On her diet:

    "I always have eaten really healthy, but I'm not a diet freak. I've done a thing where I would eat a lot of protein and vegetables," she told . "I've never been an extremist. I usually carry almonds with me. I love to have iced green tea from Starbucks or I have just a regular Japanese tea in a green bottle."
