Early Life & Biography

Calderon was born on 1 February 1972 in Santurce, Puerto Rico. He was born to Pilar Rosario Parilla and Esteban Calderon Illaraza. His father worked in the department of health while his mother was a school teacher. His parents relocated to Miami from Puerto Rico. He went to Miami Beach Senior High school.

While in High school, he interacted with a variety of cultures. He then started to work in rock bands as a drummer. Coming to realize that his parents loved Ismail Rivera, he included them in his music. He came with a style of music that incorporated dancehall, hip hop, salsa, and plena.
